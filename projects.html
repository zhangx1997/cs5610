My Projects

Lynnwood Traffic Signal Rebuild

This program will improve and restore the City of Lynnwood's city-wide Traffic Signal Systems. I am the project manager for this ongoing project. We will be improving or restoring structural and wiring components. Current project limits are from the intersections of 68th Ave W and 196th ST SW (SR 24) and 76th Ave W and 196th ST SW (SR 524). The majority of the work is at these intersections. The work for this portion of the project consists of:

  • New signal hardware
  • Pedestrian push buttons
  • ADA compliant sidewalk and ramps

43rd Ave SE/Sunset Rd Improvements

Snohomish County Public Works proposes to construct arterial improvements to approximately 1.7 miles of 43rd Ave SE from SR 524 (Maltby Road) to Sunset Rd at 180th St SE. Construction includes the following primary elements: Widen 43rd Ave SE from SR 524 north to 188th St SE to a two-lane rural section, including a separated pedestrian facility and shoulders Add approximately 1,400 linear feet of new county rural road to connect 200th St SE and 196th St SE Improve the existing urban section of 43rd Ave SE from 188th St SE north to 184th St SE Add approximately 450 linear feet of new county urban road between 184th St SE and 42nd Dr SE Install two new roundabouts on 43rd Ave SE at SR 524 and at 196th St SE Install new traffic signal and turn pockets at 180th St SE and Sunset Rd Add two new stormwater ponds and two underground stormwater chambers
